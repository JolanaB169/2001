Welcome to the 2001 Dice Game!

Game Rules

  1. Each player starts with 0 points.
  2. Choose 2 dice from the following: D3, D4, D6, D8, D10, D12, D20, D100.
  3. You may use 2 dice of the same kind or 2 different dice.
  4. The computer's choice of dice is random.
  5. If a player rolls a 7, their points are divided by 7 (integer division).
  6. If a player rolls an 11, their points are multiplied by 11.
  7. The first to reach 2001 points wins.
